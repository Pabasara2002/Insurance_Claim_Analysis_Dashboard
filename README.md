# 📊 Insurance Risk & Claims Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
Insurance companies face significant challenges in managing risk and minimizing claim losses.  
This project analyzes insurance data to identify patterns in claims, customer risk profiles, and coverage trends to support better decision-making.

An interactive dashboard was developed using Power BI to visualize key metrics and uncover insights related to claims behavior.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total claims and risk distribution  
- Identify high-risk customer segments  
- Evaluate claim patterns based on demographics and vehicle data  
- Support data-driven decision-making in insurance management

## 📈 Key Insights
- Majority of claims are associated with **private car usage (85%+)**  
- Certain car brands (e.g., Ford, Toyota) show higher claim amounts  
- Middle-aged groups (26–45) contribute most to claim value  
- Urban and highly urban zones have higher claim frequencies  
- Claim amounts show fluctuations over time, indicating risk trends  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Introduce higher premium policies for high-risk customer segments  
- Implement targeted risk assessment for specific car brands  
- Focus on urban areas for fraud detection and risk control  
- Optimize pricing strategies based on claim trends
